Champion Development

A systematic approach to identifying and enabling high-performers.

Overview

Building champions is the highest leverage activity for a leader. This play outlines the specific steps to identify potential champions, assign them increasing responsibility, and provide the necessary feedback loops.

The Play

1. Identify

Look for individuals who default to action and ask "why" effectively.

2. Test

Give a small, ambiguous problem. Measure speed and quality of resolution.

3. Enable

Provide context, resources, and air cover. Remove blockers aggressively.
