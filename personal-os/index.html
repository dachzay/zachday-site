About this project

Personal OS is a holistic approach to productivity that emphasizes system design over willpower.

It combines principles from GTD, Agile, and systems thinking to create a reliable engine for personal growth and output. The goal is to move from "busy" to "effective" by making the right actions the path of least resistance.
