How delivery works from first workflow to handoff.

This process is built for owner-led SMB teams that need implementation support, not strategy-only output.

Engagement Stages

1. Diagnose

Map existing workflow steps, constraints, and ownership, then establish baseline metrics for change tracking.

2. Build Pilot

Implement one high-impact automation with scoped inputs/outputs, QA checkpoints, and operator validation.

3. Validate

Run in production cadence, compare against baseline, and patch failure points before expanding scope.

4. Handoff

Deliver SOPs, runbooks, and role-specific operating guidance with a 90-day expansion plan.

Timeline and Responsibilities

  • Typical pilot cycle: 3 to 4 weeks from workflow diagnosis to operator handoff.
  • Your team: One owner, one operator, and access to required process/data systems.
  • My side: Workflow design, implementation, validation framework, documentation, and training.

Deliverables and Risk Controls

  • Workflow map, pilot scope document, and owner assignment.
  • Production-ready automation flow with QA checkpoints.
  • Failure logging, correction loop, and explicit guardrails for repeatability.
  • Operator SOPs and weekly review cadence.

Fit and Non-Fit

  • Best fit: owner-led SMB teams with repeatable manual tasks and readiness to implement.
  • Not a fit: teams looking only for exploratory strategy decks without execution ownership.
  • Before you submit: review common constraints in the FAQ and outcomes in Case Studies.
