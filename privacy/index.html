Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

This policy explains how data is collected and used on zachday.xyz.

Data Collected

When you submit an inquiry form, the site collects: name, email, employee range, source page path, and submission timestamp. Spam-protection and request-security systems may process technical data such as IP address and browser metadata.

How Data Is Used

Your data is used to review and respond to inquiries, operate and secure the website, prevent abuse/spam, and maintain service records.

Vendors And Processors

The site may use service providers for hosting/security, analytics/tag management, spam prevention, and lead workflow delivery. Current systems include Cloudflare services (Pages/Workers/Turnstile) and, when configured, Google Sheets webhook forwarding for lead routing.

Retention

Inquiry records are retained only as long as reasonably necessary for business follow-up, recordkeeping, and security. You can request deletion at any time using the contact information below.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of inquiry data associated with your email address. You can also opt out of non-transactional follow-up emails.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and personal data from children is not knowingly collected.
