Micro-App Factory

A repeatable template for turning small “plays” into shippable UI tools.

About this project

The Micro-App Factory is a methodology and codebase for rapidly deploying single-purpose utilities. Instead of building a monolithic application, this approach favors small, independent tools that do one thing well.

This system reduces the overhead of "starting a new project" to near zero, allowing for experimentation and quick iteration on ideas that might otherwise remain in the backlog.
