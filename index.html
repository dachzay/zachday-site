How I Work

Context Before Action

Every account interaction starts with data - health, usage, renewal proximity, stakeholder map. The 2-minute investment in context changes the entire quality of the output. Nothing is one-size-fits-all.

Infrastructure Over Hustle

Speed comes from systems, not more hours. Compounding intelligence - automated plays, persistent memory, cached portfolio data - replaces manual research with machine-speed analysis.

Expansion Is the Game

Retention is table stakes. The real scoreboard is growth. Every account analysis includes white space identification: who is missing from the conversation, who can open doors, what product solves a problem they have not articulated yet.

Validate Everything

No hallucinated data. No estimated scores presented as facts. Every number traces to a query, every contact traces to a source, every recommendation traces to evidence. If the data does not support the claim, the claim does not ship.
